# DB Navigator App 2.0 – Case Study

### Crafting your effortless travel experience with Deutsche Bahn

> A UX case study by [Amin, Anagha, Karuna, Surbhi]

---

## 🚆 Problem Statement

The **DB Navigator** is Deutsche Bahn’s official mobile app. While DB’s transport network is expansive and reliable, its app lacked the same seamlessness. Our goal? Elevate the user experience to meet the standards of Germany’s trusted rail network.

---

## 🎯 Objective

- Improve accessibility and ease of navigation
- Introduce new features like meal addition and live train tracking
- Eliminate UX blockers in booking and support flows

---

## 🧠 Design Thinking Process

We followed a structured **Design Thinking** process:

1. **Empathize** – Understand user needs and frustrations
2. **Define** – Frame core usability issues
3. **Ideate** – Generate potential design solutions
4. **Prototype** – Build low-fidelity interfaces
5. **Test** – Gather user feedback
6. **Iterate** – Refine based on testing

---

## 🧍‍♀️ User Persona

**Commuters & occasional travelers** who rely on the app for quick bookings and tracking loved ones’ journeys.

---

## 😫 Pain Points

- Poor ticket access navigation
- Complex seat selection
- Login required for basic access
- No meal or live train status features
- No real-time agent support

---

## 💡 Solution

- Clearer navigation structure
- Optional login for browsing and booking
- Feature to **add meals** during checkout
- Live train tracking for loved ones
- “Connect to Agent” flow for instant support

---

## 🧭 Information Architecture

We restructured the app to simplify user flows:

- Home
- Book Tickets → Select Train → Seat → Add Meal → Payment
- Your Journeys
- Live Status
- Contact Support → Connect to Agent
- Saved Tickets

---

## 🎨 Visual Language

- **Color palette** inspired by DB’s bold red
- **Typography** for readability across devices
- **Iconography** consistent with iOS & Android guidelines
- **Grid System**: 4-column structure for alignment

## 🧪 Lo-Fi Prototypes & Testing

We explored **three major flows**:

### 1. Ticket Booking

Clear steps from train selection to payment.

### 2. Train Live Status

Track and share train updates in real-time.

### 3. Agent Support

Instant messaging for help regarding bookings.

### UT1 Findings:

- Users struggled to find purchased tickets
- Seat selection required too much scrolling
- Login felt unnecessary

**Changes Made:**

- Simplified login flow
- Redesigned seat selection
- Optional login during booking

---

## 📱 Final UI Designs

### ✨ Onboarding Screens

Help users quickly understand app value.

### 🎟️ Booking Flow

Simple and intuitive journey from search to confirmation.

### 🍱 Meal Addition

New feature to add meals while booking.

### 📡 Live Train Status

Live updates and sharing options for loved ones.

### 🤝 Agent Support

Quickly connect with a real human when needed.
